Frequently Asked Questions:

Can I make an order without an account?

No, you have to register to make an order, or to login to your account.

How can I pay?

The payment can be done by cash when you pickup or get your food delivered. Or else, you can use card payment. We accepted MaestoCard, Visa

I have ordered something by mistake. Can I cancel it?

You have to immediately contact the restaurant you have ordered from, and if they have not started preparing your order, you would get a refund, however if they have started preparing your order, we cannot give you any refund.

When can I order food?

Hungry.com.mt is always available however you can pickup/ get your food delivered every day from 12:00 till 23:00.

My order has not arrived yet. What should I do?

You can contact us via our contact us page, call us on +356 12345678 or contact the restaurant you have ordered from.
